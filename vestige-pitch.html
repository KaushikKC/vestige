The Problem

Token launches are broken.

Insider-heavy launches, bot-driven rug pulls, and zero mobile UX leave retail traders with no fair entry point.

The Solution

An inverted bonding curve that rewards early holders.

Vestige starts tokens at their maximum price and decreases it as supply is sold — creating a built-in incentive for early participation and eliminating the insider advantage.

Once the SOL graduation target is hit, the token graduates automatically to Raydium CPMM — giving it real on-chain liquidity.

How It Works

01

Creator Launches a Token

02

Traders Buy & Sell via Bonding Curve

price = pMax − (pMax − pMin) × (totalBaseSold / supply). Every buy pushes cost down for the next buyer.
